2. Pengertian because,so,altough beserta contohya


Because = Karna
So = Begitu, Begini, jadi (Tergantung Kalimat)
Although = Walaupun

Contohnya:
I do this because i love you (Aku melakukan ini karna aku mencintaimu)
i do this because you say so (Aku melakukan ini karna kau mengatakannya begitu)
So, you're the guy, huh? (Jadi, kamu orangnya, hah?)
Although he is rude, I think he still love you (Meskipun ia kasar, saya pikir dia masih mencintaimu. ia kasar, saya pikir dia masih mencintaimu)

Semoga membantu :))


- because, for giving reasons, 
- so, for talking about results or purposes,
- and although, for unexpected or different information. 

she'd like to go to Bali, because its beaches are pretty cool.
(the second clause explains the reason She wants to go to bali)

Although he doesn’t like camping, he goes to lots of music festivals.
(the speaker thinks it’s unusual to go to music festivals if you don’t like camping)

We need to arrive early so (that) we can get a good place.
(the second clause shows the purpose of the first clause; that is optional)
